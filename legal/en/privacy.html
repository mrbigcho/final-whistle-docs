1. Who we are

Final Whistle ("the Service", "we", "us") is a tournament and club management app operated by Dongne, an independent developer based in the Republic of Korea. Contact: mrbigcho@gmail.com.

This policy explains what personal information we collect when you use Final Whistle, how we use it, and the rights you have over it. It applies globally.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Account information — email address, display name, and profile image. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive the identifier and (with your permission) name and email from those providers.
  • User content — clubs, tournaments, matches, results, and any text or images you create inside the Service.
  • Communications — messages you send to us for support.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device and diagnostic data — device model, operating system, app version, crash reports, and non-precise performance metrics (via Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics).
  • Push notification token — a Firebase Cloud Messaging token tied to your installation, used to deliver notifications.
  • Usage events — anonymized product events (e.g., screens visited, buttons tapped) for product analytics.

We do not collect: precise location, health data, financial account numbers, contact lists, photos outside of what you actively upload, or advertising identifiers.

4. Legal bases (EEA / UK users)

  • Contract — to provide the Service you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests — to keep the Service secure, debug errors, and improve features. You may object at any time.
  • Consent — for optional push notifications, where required by law. You can withdraw consent in the app or OS settings.
  • Legal obligation — where processing is required by law.

5. How we share information

We share personal information only with the following categories of recipients:

  • Service providers (processors) acting on our behalf under contract:
    • Google / Firebase (authentication, Crashlytics, Analytics, Cloud Messaging, hosting)
    • Apple (Sign in with Apple, push delivery via APNs)
    • Cloud infrastructure providers for hosting (e.g., Railway, Cloudflare)
  • Other users — content you publish in a public club or tournament (such as results and standings) is visible to other participants and, where applicable, to anyone with a shared link.
  • Legal authorities — where we are legally compelled to disclose information (subpoena, court order).
  • Corporate transactions — in the unlikely event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, data may be transferred subject to this policy.

We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties.

6. International data transfers

Final Whistle operates globally. Your data may be processed in countries other than your own, including the United States and the Republic of Korea. When we transfer personal data out of the EEA, UK, or other jurisdictions with transfer restrictions, we rely on recognized safeguards such as the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses.

7. Data retention

  • Account data — kept while your account is active, and deleted within 30 days of account deletion except where longer retention is required by law.
  • User content — clubs or tournaments you created remain accessible to other members after you leave unless you delete them before leaving. You can request deletion of your content at any time.
  • Diagnostic data — retained for up to 90 days for crash analysis.
  • Analytics data — retained under Firebase Analytics defaults (currently 2 months to 14 months).

8. Your rights

Subject to your jurisdiction, you have the following rights. To exercise any of them, email mrbigcho@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

  • Access — a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — fix inaccurate data.
  • Deletion — delete your account and associated personal data.
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Restriction / Objection — limit how we process your data, or object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, for processing based on consent.
  • Complaint — lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

California residents (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have the additional rights to know, delete, correct, and limit the use of sensitive personal information. We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

9. Children

Final Whistle is not directed to children under 14. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 14 without verifiable parental consent (or the local minimum age, whichever is higher — 13 in the US under COPPA, 16 in some EEA countries). If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

10. Security

We use industry-standard measures including TLS for data in transit, encryption at rest where supported by our providers, and access controls. No system is perfectly secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. When we make material changes, we will notify you in the app or by email at least 30 days before the change takes effect. The "Effective date" at the top shows when the current version was published.
